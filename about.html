Our Story

Vulaegis was founded in 2025 in Nairobi, Kenya, by Johnson Ruhiu and two visionary co-founders (space for names and bios). Our mission is to set new standards in industrial safety for Africa and the world. From our Kenya headquarters and subbranches in Nairobi, Eldoret, and Mombasa, we are expanding across Africa and globally, delivering world-class safety consulting, audits, and training.

Our Values

  • Prevention First: We proactively identify and mitigate risks before they become incidents.
  • Human-Centered Design: Safety systems that work for real people in real workplaces.
  • Continuous Improvement: We constantly refine our approach to keep you ahead of the curve.
