Our Journey

Founded in 2025 by industrial safety veterans, Vulaegis emerged from a simple mission: transform workplace safety from compliance obligation to strategic advantage.

What began as a three-person consultancy now serves over 200 industrial facilities nationwide, maintaining a perfect record of zero client fatalities and reducing recordable incidents by an average of 78%.
