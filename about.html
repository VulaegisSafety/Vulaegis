About Vulaegis

Founded in 2025 | CEO: Johnson Ruhiu

Our Mission

Vulaegis was founded in 2025 in Nairobi, Kenya, by Johnson Ruhiu with a vision to set new standards in industrial safety across Africa and the world. We deliver world-class safety consulting, audits, and training, helping organizations prevent incidents and foster a culture of safety.

Our Growth

From our Kenya headquarters and subbranches in Nairobi, Eldoret, and Mombasa, we are expanding throughout Africa and aim to become a global safety leader.
